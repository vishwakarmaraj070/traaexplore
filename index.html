About Us

TRAAEXPLORE is a travel and event management company based in Bangalore, combining creativity and expertise with a fresh and dynamic touch.
Whatever you can imagine, we can make it happen in India and international.
Organizing an event has much in common with riding a wave: you need to trust your instinct, but you also need the right skills. We are well-versed in domestic and international event and group planning.
Through our destination management expertise, we streamline all travel, activity and hospitality arrangements to provide efficient, culturally-sensitive experiences anywhere in India and the world. We have proactive team, reliable partnerships with airline & DMC support all over the globe.
